Badings! talks all about the presence of LGBTQ+ in our society today. It covers topics such as an introduction to LGBTQ+, a brief overview of sexual orientation in general, how you can raise awareness for people in the LGBTQ+ community, and firsthand experiences from the creators of the website. Overall, it aims to help you properly understand what LGBTQ+ actually is, what issues are presented to this group of people, and what you can do to promote acceptance of LGBTQ+ in your community.
