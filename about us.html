I'm Tres Saluta, co-creator of Badings! I'm a part of the LGBTQ+ Community. My co-creator and I decided to create a website like this because as a gay person that only my friends know, and I want to educate people to be more accepting of LGBTQ+ and that this is not just a trend. It was very hard for me to come out to my friends initially because I was afraid of not being accepted, but here I am. I want to make sure nobody else has to feel what I felt during the time I was struggling.
